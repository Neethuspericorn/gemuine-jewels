Who Are We?

Since 1968, H. Lipschitz & Sons has been a trusted jewelry source for thousands of satisfied customers. The family-owned business is known for the quality & perfection of the jewelry it sells, the custom design service offered, and the customer service that clients keep coming back for. Since 1991, Abraham Lipschitz has been manufacturing, cutting and selling strictly high-end diamonds. To the extent that many of his diamonds have been featured in Sotheby's and Christie's Auction Houses, which only promote the best quality in the industry. His expertise in diamonds has brought him customers from all over the globe, including Cartier, Graff and many other luxury brands.

Due to the ongoing trend in 2016, Abraham Lipschitz launched Gemuine, introducing their new line of Lab-Grown diamond jewelry, expanding their offerings to a new clientele who could now benefit from the quality, service, and expertise the company prides itself on.
