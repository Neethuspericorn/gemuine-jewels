The Details
Behind the Diamond

Lab-grown diamonds are just that – diamonds grown inside a lab. With revolutionary technology that replicates the natural diamond growing process, organically-grown and lab-grown diamonds share identical

  • Physical Properties
  • Chemical Properties
  • Optical Properties
  • Grading & Certification Systems

The difference?
The process.

Both mined & lab-created diamonds share the same process – and costs – involved in cutting, polishing, and inspection. The lab-grown variety skips the extensive mining process that organically-grown diamonds go through – Which allows them to be sold at a significantly lower cost.

In a world of rising costs and standards, lab-grown diamonds introduce an era of Beauty that doesn’t break the bank.
